Who Is Hailey?

Hailey Collin is a junior UX/UI designer based in Minnesota. She attended the University Of Minnesota bootcamp for UX/UI design from March 2024-September 2024 and is excited to bust into the world of technological design.

As someone who has always had a passion for creativity Hailey is inspired by this field of work and can’t wait to grow within it.

Before entering the field of UX/UI Design Hailey was a licensed cosmetologist specializing in chemical services and color theory. For the last nearly ten years Hailey has also been working in the hospitality industry and has worked her way into management.

This has all helped her with learning how to empathize with others needs and desires.

Hailey has also spent nearly ten years working in a restaurant and has moved into management. Through this she has learned how to empathize with others. She watches over a large staff of over ninety employees and will manage over forty in one shift. This job has also taught her how important communication is and time management because it’s not just her time she's managing, it’s also everyone else's.

Her Skills

User Research:

During the research phase she spends time on making and sending out surveys as well as constructing an interview plan. Hailey will pin point her target audience ask some screening questions and then begin the interview via zoom or in person.

User Empathy:

When it comes to showing user empathy Hailey likes to start by affinity diagramming to find common denominators in what interviewees are saying and feeling, She then moves into empathy maps and user persona’s to make one person that is representing multiple people.

Qualitative Interviews:

Doing interviews is a crucial part of research. When doing interviews Hailey likes to start with screening questions to gain insight on if an interviewee has ever had any experiences with the type of thing she is working on.

Wireframing:

Hailey has had to wireframe and prototype a few projects now and she is becoming adept in using auto-layout to make everything line up within in frames properly while still maintaining an aesthetically pleasing look.

Styling:

Hailey really enjoys working on visually pleasing designs. She has a soft spot for warm color palettes and rich tones so when she’s able to use them she goes for it. She usually will come up with a few different versions and see what others respond to best and then make changes accordingly. Her background as a color specialist in hair helps with her comprehension regarding color.
